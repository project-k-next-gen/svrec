About Us

Established in 2007, SVR Engineering College (SVREC) is a premier autonomous institution located on NH-18, just 7 km from Nandyal, Andhra Pradesh. The college is spread across a scenic 13-acre green campus, offering a peaceful and inspiring learning environment. SVREC is affiliated to JNTUA, Ananthapuramu, approved by AICTE, and accredited by NAAC. The ECE and CSE departments are NBA accredited, and the institute has been granted Autonomous Status from 2024–25. With modern infrastructure, advanced laboratories, smart classrooms, and a central library, SVREC delivers quality, industry-focused education. Equal importance is given to academics, innovation, extracurricular activities, and overall student development.
